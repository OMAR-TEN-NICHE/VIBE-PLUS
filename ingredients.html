L-THEANINE
From: Camellia sinensis (Green Tea)
Typical dose: 100–200mg per patch
An amino acid found naturally in green tea leaves. Works synergistically with caffeine to produce calm, focused alertness without the jittery side effects.
⚙ Mechanism
Increases alpha brain wave activity — the same mental state experienced during meditation. Boosts GABA, serotonin, and dopamine neurotransmitters to reduce anxiety while maintaining alertness.

GINKGO BILOBA
From: Ginkgo biloba tree leaf extract
Typical dose: 120–240mg standardized extract
One of the world's oldest living tree species. Used in traditional medicine for over 5,000 years. Rich in flavonoids and terpenoids — powerful antioxidants for the brain.
⚙ Mechanism
Dilates blood vessels and improves circulation to the brain. Inhibits platelet aggregation, increasing oxygen and glucose delivery to neurons. Also acts as a potent free-radical scavenger protecting brain cells.
✦ Benefits
Memory Recall Brain Circulation Mental Clarity Neuroprotection

BACOPA MONNIERI
From: Brahmi herb (Ayurvedic medicine)
Typical dose: 150–300mg per patch
An Ayurvedic herb used for centuries in India to enhance learning, memory, and cognitive performance. Particularly effective for students and knowledge workers.
⚙ Mechanism
Bacosides A & B repair damaged neurons by enhancing kinase activity and protein synthesis. Increases dendrite branching, literally growing more neural connections. Also modulates serotonin and dopamine systems.
✦ Benefits
Long-term Memory Learning Speed Stress Reduction Neural Repair

LION'S MANE
From: Hericium erinaceus mushroom
Typical dose: 300–500mg extract
A unique medicinal mushroom that looks like a white waterfall. The only food source known to stimulate NGF (Nerve Growth Factor) production — essentially fertilizer for your brain.
⚙ Mechanism
Hericenones and erinacines penetrate the blood-brain barrier and stimulate production of NGF and BDNF. These proteins promote the growth, maintenance, and survival of neurons — directly building brain capacity over time.
✦ Benefits
Brain Neurogenesis Focus Depth Anti-Anxiety Long-term Cognition

ENERGY
CAFFEINE (GUARANA)
From: Paullinia cupana seed extract
Typical dose: 50–100mg slow-release
Unlike synthetic caffeine, guarana-sourced caffeine is naturally bound to tannins, causing a slower, more sustained release — delivering clean energy over 4–6 hours with no sudden crash.
⚙ Mechanism
Blocks adenosine receptors in the brain (adenosine causes sleepiness). This triggers dopamine and norepinephrine release, increasing alertness, reaction time, and physical performance without overstimulating the nervous system.
✦ Benefits
Sustained Alertness No Crash Faster Reaction Fat Metabolism

ENERGY
TAURINE
Amino sulfonic acid (conditionally essential)
Typical dose: 500–1000mg per patch
One of the most abundant amino acids in the brain and heart. Often called the "wonder molecule" for its wide-ranging physiological benefits. Naturally found in meat, fish, and dairy.
⚙ Mechanism
Regulates calcium signaling in cells, enhancing mitochondrial energy production. Acts as an osmoregulator, protecting cells from oxidative stress. In the brain, modulates GABA receptors to calm the nervous system while boosting physical endurance.
✦ Benefits
Cell Energy Heart Health Endurance Antioxidant

GREEN TEA EXTRACT
From: Camellia sinensis (standardized 50% EGCG)
Typical dose: 200–400mg extract
Concentrated extract of green tea, standardized to 50% EGCG (Epigallocatechin gallate) — the most potent catechin and one of nature's most studied antioxidants.
⚙ Mechanism
EGCG inhibits COMT enzyme, prolonging norepinephrine activity in the brain and fat cells. This mildly increases thermogenesis and mental energy. Combined with caffeine and L-theanine, it creates a powerful synergistic focus stack.

VITAMINS
VITAMIN B COMPLEX
B1, B2, B3, B5, B6, B9, B12
Typical dose: 100% RDV per patch
The complete B-vitamin family, essential for converting food into brain fuel. Deficiency in any B vitamin directly impairs cognitive function, mood, and energy production.
⚙ Mechanism
B vitamins act as coenzymes in ATP (cellular energy) synthesis. B6, B9 & B12 regulate homocysteine metabolism — high homocysteine is linked to brain fog and poor memory. B12 is critical for myelin sheath maintenance, which speeds nerve signal transmission.
✦ Benefits
Energy Metabolism Nerve Function Mood Regulation Red Blood Cell Production

VITAMINS
MAGNESIUM L-THREONATE
Magtein® — MIT-developed brain magnesium
Typical dose: 144mg elemental Mg
The only form of magnesium proven to cross the blood-brain barrier efficiently. Developed by MIT researchers specifically to elevate brain magnesium levels, which decline with stress and aging.
⚙ Mechanism
Increases synaptic density in the hippocampus — the brain's memory center. Regulates NMDA receptors involved in learning and memory formation. Also controls cortisol response, reducing the cognitive impact of stress.
✦ Benefits
Memory Formation Stress Resilience Deep Sleep Synaptic Density

VITAMINS
N-ACETYL CARNITINE
ALCAR — Acetylated form of L-Carnitine
Typical dose: 500–1000mg per patch
The acetylated form of L-Carnitine that crosses the blood-brain barrier. Acts as both a cellular energy transporter and a precursor to acetylcholine — the brain's primary learning neurotransmitter.
⚙ Mechanism
Shuttles long-chain fatty acids into mitochondria for ATP energy production. The acetyl group donates to acetyl-CoA, directly boosting acetylcholine synthesis. This dual action powers both cellular metabolism and neurotransmitter production simultaneously.
✦ Benefits
Brain Energy Acetylcholine Fat Burning Mental Endurance

RED GINSENG
From: Panax ginseng C.A. Meyer (6-year root)
Typical dose: 200–400mg standardized extract
Korean Red Ginseng — steamed and dried for 9 times to maximize ginsenoside concentration. Used for over 2,000 years in East Asian medicine as the ultimate adaptogen for resilience and vitality.
⚙ Mechanism
Ginsenosides Rg1 and Rb1 modulate the HPA axis, regulating cortisol and adrenaline response to stress. Enhances nitric oxide production for better circulation. Also activates AMPK pathway — the master regulator of cellular energy balance.
✦ Benefits
Stress Adaptation Immune Support Physical Stamina Hormonal Balance
